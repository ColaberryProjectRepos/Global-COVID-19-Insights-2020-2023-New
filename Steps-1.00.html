Problem Statement 1

Despite ongoing global efforts to combat COVID-19, the situation remains a pressing concern. As of the year 2023, there have been a staggering 771,151,224 confirmed cases of COVID-19, resulting in 6,960,783 deaths, reported to the World Health Organization. The magnitude of this crisis underscores the urgent need for effective measures to mitigate its impact. Additionally, approximately 13.5 billion vaccine doses have been administered, offering a glimmer of hope and highlighting the critical importance of accelerating vaccination campaigns worldwide. We should emphasize the challenges posed by the persisting COVID-19 pandemic and the essential role that vaccination efforts play in addressing it.
