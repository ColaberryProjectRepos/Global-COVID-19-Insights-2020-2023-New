Incorporating a Canvas Background 14

  1. While in the report view, navigate to the Visualization pane, then access the Format section. Next, go to Canvas Background and choose Image. Click on the Browse option, then select an image from the 'Amazon Sales Project' folder and open it.
  2. In the Image Fit section, opt for 'Fit.'
  3. Adjust the transparency level to 88%.
