Therefore, selecting KPIs involves thinking about their relevance and applicability over an extended period. Ensuring that the data source is trustworthy and that it undergoes regular validation and cleansing processes can be a challenging endeavor in itself.

2.Chart Selection: When dealing with complex and multifaceted datasets, it can be challenging to determine which types of charts will effectively communicate the data's nuances and insights. Selecting charts that are too complex for the audience to interpret can lead to miscommunication, while overly simplistic charts may fail to convey the depth of the data. Effective data visualization is about telling a compelling story with data. Selecting charts that support the narrative you want to convey can be challenging, especially when the story is multifaceted and requires multiple visualizations. It involves a thoughtful evaluation of data characteristics, audience needs, storytelling objectives, and design considerations.

Finding a pre-existing continent table that precisely matched the country list within my dataset proved to be a complex task. As a result, I encountered instances where manual entry of continent names next to certain countries was necessary, given that the original dataset included regions that are not conventionally regarded as independent countries, often representing islands or territories.
