Modifying the dataset and Creating Calendar Table 10

  1. Convert the "Date Reported" column to the appropriate date format.
  2. With the date column selected in the Fields pane, you should see the "Format" option in the Visualizations pane on the right side of the screen.
  3. Click on the paint roller icon to expand the "Format" options. Select "MM/DD/YYYY".
  4.  After selecting the desired date format or specifying a custom format, Power BI will automatically apply the format to the date column in the visual or table.

Creating Calendar table:

                                                                     

  1. Navigate to the Modeling Tab and select "New Table."
  2. Enter the provided DAX formula below in the formula bar, then click the checkmark icon to confirm and save it.
Calendar = ADDCOLUMNS (CALENDAR (Date(Year(Min('WHO-COVID-19-global-data'[Date_reported])),Month(Min('WHO-COVID-19-global-data'[Date_reported])),DAY(Min('WHO-COVID-19-global-data'[Date_reported]))), Date(Year(Max('WHO-COVID-19-global-data'[Date_reported])),Month(Max('WHO-COVID-19-global-data'[Date_reported])),DAY(Max('WHO-COVID-19-global-data'[Date_reported])))),
"Year Quarter",
IF(
    YEAR([Date]) > 2020 || (YEAR([Date]) = 2020 && QUARTER([Date]) > 1),
    FORMAT([Date], "YYYY") & "/Qtr" & FORMAT([Date], "Q"),
    BLANK()
   ),
"Year", FORMAT ( [Date], "YYYY" ))
