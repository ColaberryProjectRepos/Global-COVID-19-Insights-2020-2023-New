Importing Data to Power BI desktop 9

                                               

Follow these instructions:
  1. Launch Power BI Desktop.
  2. Navigate to the 'Get data' ribbon.
  3. Choose 'Text/CSV' and click 'connect.'
  4. Locate and open the 'Global COVID19 Insights' Excel file.
  5. Click 'Open' to load the file.
  6. You will see a data preview.
Refer to the GIF below for a visual guide on how to proceed.
