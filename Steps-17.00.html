Adding Refreshed Date 17
  1. Click on the report, then choose "Visual Card" from the visualizations pane.
  2. From the Refresh Date Table, drag and drop the "Date Refresh" into the fields value box.
  3. Under the Formatting Tab --> Visual --> Callout Value, configure as follows:
    • Font: Arial, Size: 13, Color: #118DFF
    • Category Label: Turned Off
      General:
    • Size: Height: 83, Width: 217
    • Position: Horizontal:1061, Vertical: 34
    • Title: Enabled, Type: "Refreshed Date", Font: Arial, Size: 14, Text Color: #118DFF, Background Color: #FFFFFF, Horizontal Alignment: Center
