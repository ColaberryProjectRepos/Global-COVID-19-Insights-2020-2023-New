Positive Insights 5


  • In 2023, there has been a remarkable 76% decrease in the number of new COVID-19 deaths compared to the year 2022. This significant reduction in deaths clearly demonstrates substantial progress in controlling the impact of the pandemic.

  • In the year 2023, there have been a total of 38,767,038 new cases of COVID-19 reported. The Case Fatality Rate (CFR) for the entire year is 1%. Additionally, in the first quarter (quarter 1) of 2023, there were 31,662,627 new cases reported, and the CFR for this quarter was also 1%.
