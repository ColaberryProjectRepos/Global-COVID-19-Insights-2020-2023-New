Negative Insights 6


  • In 2023, there was a significant 94% increase in new COVID-19 cases in Asia compared to 2022. In contrast, Africa experienced a much smaller 3% increase in new cases during the same period. There was a case Fatality Rate (CFR) for COVID-19 in Asia was 1%. In contrast, the CFR for COVID-19 in Africa in the same year was 2%.

  • China experienced a significant increase in both the number of reported COVID-19 cases and deaths in 2023 compared to the previous year, 2022. The number of new COVID-19-related deaths in China in 2023 was 68,992, which marked a 284% increase compared to 2022. The number of new cases in 2023 increased by 162% compared to the previous year (2022).
