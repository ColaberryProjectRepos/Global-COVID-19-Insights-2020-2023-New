Incorporating a Title 16

To insert a title, follow these steps:
  1. Access the "Home" tab on the Power BI ribbon and choose the "Text box" icon to place a text box on your report canvas.
  2. Click inside the text box and enter "Global COVID-19 Insights."
    Adjust the Font to Arial, Font Size to 24, and Color to #000000.
  3. Once you've selected the text box, locate the "Format" tab within the "Visualizations" pane on the right. Then, click on General and set the properties as follows:
    • Size: Height = 115, Width = 328
    • Position: Horizontal = 208, Vertical = 2
  4. Refer to the instructions provided in the accompanying GIF for further guidance.
